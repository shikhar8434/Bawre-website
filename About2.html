BAWRE THEATRE

We love theatre

Bawre theatre was founded in the year 2008 with a dream to draw attention of the society towards the least talked about problems, Entertain people and promote the art of Dramatics.We believe that theatre teaches us how to deal with different situations, How to develop good communication skills ,How to challenge the existing problems in our society and provide possible solutions for them and the most important lesson how to interact with society.
